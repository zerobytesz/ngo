About Us

EmpowerHope Foundation, founded in 2025 by Raghav Tyagi, is dedicated to creating sustainable social change in underserved communities. Our work focuses on education, healthcare, and economic empowerment.

Our Mission: To empower individuals and families through life-changing opportunities and support, fostering self-reliance and hope.

Our Core Values: Compassion, integrity, inclusiveness, collaboration, and accountability guide everything we do.

What We Do

  • Organize free health camps and wellness programs
  • Provide school supplies, scholarships, and mentoring
  • Conduct skill-building and vocational training workshops
  • Engage in environmental sustainability projects
  • Partner with local organizations for wider impact
